Universal Technical Institute (2014-2015)

Sacramento,CA

A automotive program that spanned 1.5 years, learning every bit and piece about a vehicle and how it runs. Heating and air conditioning systems, engine performance, suspension components and everything inbetween, it was nothing short of in depth and intense. With amazing instrcutors with a plethora of information and knowledge, each having their own personality in teching and diagnoses, it certainly allowed me to develop my own abilities when approaching a problem. Following up the core program I continued to specialize with Toyota manufacture vehicles, this was a 3 month program.
