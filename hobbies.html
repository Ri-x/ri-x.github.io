Track riding

As time went on the sensation of riding on the street no longer scracthed that itch and I had to endeavor in another niche. Riding on the track was an eye opener, my ego may have been inflated by the time I hit the track and was I humbled. Cycling

Cycling is hobby that allows me the explore the crevices present around the bay area. Mainly sticking to the trails around the South Bay, nevertheless, it has introduced me to areas in which I would have never known. From the Guadalupe trail to the Bay Trail, from Downtown San Jose to Palo Alto, cycling has given me not only great exercise but that ability to explore. I desire to eventually make my way towards criterium cycling events, this would be the next step towards a harsher and much more competitive atmosphere.
